COVID-19 Patient Statement

We continue to remain open and providing our services, but our main priority is keeping our team and clients safe and healthy. We have high standards of hygiene as a matter of course, but will be taking increased measures to make our clinics as clean and safe as we possibly can.

Can we please request that patients who will still be visiting the clinic follow the Government guidelines on hand washing or sanitising immediately prior to attending.

If you are unable to attend because you need to self-isolate, or are in one of the identified at risk groups, please ring to cancel your appointment. We will be contacting all patients booked in as a matter of course, so don’t worry if you can’t get through. For those patients still able to attend, we may request that we move your appointment as we will need to restructure our clinics. Please bear with us on this point.

From all of us here at Chase Podiatry, we sincerely hope you do stay well and look forward to seeing you all again in the not too distant future.
